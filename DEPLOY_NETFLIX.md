## Quick Steps to Deploy Your Netflix Project

### Step 1: Go to Vercel
1. Open: https://vercel.com
2. Make sure you're logged in (you should already be since you deployed your portfolio)

### Step 2: Import Your Netflix Repository
1. Click **"Add New..."** â†’ **"Project"**
2. You'll see a list of your GitHub repositories
3. Find your **Netflix project** repository
4. Click **"Import"**

### Step 3: Configure (Vercel Auto-Detects)
Vercel will automatically detect:
- **Framework**: React/Next.js (depending on your project)
- **Build Command**: Usually `npm run build` or auto-detected
- **Output Directory**: Usually `dist`, `build`, or `.next`

**If it's a React app (not Next.js):**
- **Build Command**: `npm run build`
- **Output Directory**: `build` or `dist`

**If it's a Next.js app:**
- Vercel will auto-detect everything âœ…

### Step 4: Deploy
1. Click **"Deploy"**
2. Wait 1-2 minutes
3. Your Netflix project will be live!

### Step 5: Get Your Live URL
- Once deployed, you'll get a URL like: `netflix-project-xyz.vercel.app`
- Copy this URL - you'll need it for your portfolio!
